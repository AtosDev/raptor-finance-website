About Raptor

Raptor is an autonomous, anonymous yield and liquidity generation protocol that aims to directly reward holders whilst concurrently increasing liquidity and generating donations for a community-governed selection of charities.

We impose a 7% fee to all transactions which is locked inside the contract. A share of this fees is distributed to holders who decided to stake their tokens, the other part is sent to a public, auditable donation wallet which is used to contribute to environmental causes around the world.

Donations are announced at least 24 hours prior and are done in such a way that the token price impact is minimal.

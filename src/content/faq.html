How do I buy Raptor Token?

Raptor is launched on the Binance Smart Chain. This means that in order to buy Raptor, you need the Binance Coin (BNB). You can buy BNB from Trustwallet, Binance Smart Chain wallet or an exchange like Binance itself, Crypto.com, Whitebit or similar. Once you have BNB, send them to your wallet and head over to PancakeSwap to buy Raptor for BNB.

Why is the price in my Trustwallet sometimes incorrect?

Trustwallet derives its pricing from CoinMarketCap embedded API and this service has been known to have incorrect and inflated pricing on smaller tokens. One reason for this is that they don't update the price as frequently to save resources.
